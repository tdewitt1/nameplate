Taylor DeWitt

Designer & Student

Taylor DeWitt is a Senior at Eastern Washington University, originally from Spokane, Washington. This Fall she will be complete her B.A. in Visual Communication Design with a minor in Art History. During her time at EWU, she has begun work as a graphic designer for the dean of students office as well as for FosteringWA, creating promotional materials for on campus and off campus use. As a graphic Designer, Taylor has 2 business logos, one trademarked, as well as 2 t-shirt designs and many advertisements under her belt. Recently, she has had work showcased on the Creative Action Network for their Recovering the Classics line. When Taylor isn't studying or working, she is sleeping, playing video games, or otherwise just relaxing with her boyfriend, Ryan.
